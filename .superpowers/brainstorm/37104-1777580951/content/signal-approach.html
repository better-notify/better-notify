The concept

Click Send and a signal pulse emits from the source node. The pulse travels along the wire to each transport — the line glows and blinks as the signal passes through. On failure (red flash), the pulse bounces to the next transport. On success (green glow), it flows through to the result node. Toggle transports healthy/unhealthy to see different paths light up. Switch strategies to see how the wiring changes — parallel strategies fan out all wires simultaneously, race shows competing pulses.
